Project Details

An all-inclusive School Management System featuring dedicated portals for students, parents, teachers, and administrators, designed to automate academic and administrative workflows. This system supports seamless student progression based on exam results and fee payments, with overpayment carryover and repeat exam management. Integrated SMS and email APIs enable real-time notifications for important updates, payment confirmations, and academic reports, fostering effective communication among all school stakeholders.

With robust payment solutions, the system allows secure and efficient fee transactions, while intuitive user interfaces across portals ensure transparency and accessibility in record-keeping and interactions

Since implementing the School Management System developed by Allan, our administrative tasks have become significantly more efficient. The automated student progression, integrated attendance tracking, and real-time communication features have greatly improved our workflow. The system has not only streamlined fee collection and financial reporting but also enhanced communication between parents, teachers, and administrators. It’s an invaluable tool that has transformed the way we manage and interact with our students and staff.

Dr. Joseph Munyua

Principal, Bishop Njuguna Training College

The School Management System offers a customizable academic calendar and timetable feature, allowing administrators to set term dates, holidays, and exams, while teachers can create and share timetables accessible to students and parents. An integrated attendance module lets teachers record daily attendance, viewable by parents in real-time, with automated absence alerts to enhance student accountability.

The system also includes an assignment and grading tool where teachers can upload assignments, track grades, and generate report cards, accessible to students and parents. Additionally, robust financial tracking and customizable dashboards provide administrators with insights into fee collection, outstanding balances, and expenses, supporting effective financial planning and decision-making.
